Black Marble Stone

KBG stone is a company that specializes in the sale of natural marble slabs and tiles. Marble is a type of metamorphic rock that is composed of recrystallized carbonate minerals, typically calcite or dolomite. It is highly valued for its unique beauty and durability, making it a popular choice for a wide range of applications, including flooring, countertops, wall cladding, and decorative sculptures

  • we take pride in offering a wide selection of high-quality natural marble slabs in a variety of colors
  • We source our marble from quarries all over the world
  • Our experienced team of stone experts works closely with each client to help them choose the perfect marble slab for their project

Our experienced team of stone experts works closely with each client to help them choose the perfect marble slab for their project. We offer personalized consultations to help our customers select the right color and finish to complement their design vision and budget.
